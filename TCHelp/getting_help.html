Getting Help

This section gives a short overview about how to invoke the Online Help and where to get additional help on the Internet.

Keyword Help

Working with the editor of TeXnicCenter, online help on a (La)TeX keyword can be invoked by simply placing the text cursor on the word and pressing the button 'F1'. TeXnicCenter will look in the help file index for an entry matching the marked word and display the appropriate text.

Additional Help

When facing problems using TeXnicCenter, try to solve the problems by reading this online help and the List of Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) about TeXnicCenter on the TeXnicCenter homepage.

If both sources do not provide any solution for problem given, then please feel free to contact the TeXnicCenter Users Group, organised as a mailing list on the Internet. Search the archive of old messages or post a question to the list. Many users of TeXnicCenter and of course the developers are members of the list and they will try to help to solve problems.

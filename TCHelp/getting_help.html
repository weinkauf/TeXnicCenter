Getting Help

The following section gives a short overview about where and how to get help.

Keyword Help

If you are working in the editor of TeXnicCenter and you would like to get help for a (La)TeX keyword, simply place the text cursor on the word you would like to get help about and press 'F1'. TeXnicCenter will look in the help file for an index entry matching the specified word an display the belonging topic.

Further Help

If you are having problems using TeXnicCenter, try to solve your problems reading this help and the list of frequently asked questions (FAQ) about TeXnicCenter on our homepage.

If both sources does not provide any solution for your problem then you should contact the TeXnicCenter-Users Group, which is a mailing list on the web. You can search the archive of existing messages or post a question to the list. Many users of TeXnicCenter and also the developers are members of the list and they will try to help you to solve your problems.
